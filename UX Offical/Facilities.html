Our Facilities


Welcome to Reza Resort, an exquisite destination where luxury meets tranquility. Our resort hotel boasts a wide range of exceptional facilities designed to cater to your every need. We have our remarkable amenities, which include a pool with a jacuzzi, rejuvenating spa, and a state-of-the-art gym.

Reza Spa is a haven of tranquility and rejuvenation nestled within a breathtaking natural landscape. This personalized resort hotel spa is a sanctuary designed to cater to your every need, offering an unparalleled experience of relaxation and wellness. From the moment you step foot into Reza Spa, you are enveloped in an atmosphere of serenity, with elegant interiors and warm, attentive staff who are dedicated to ensuring your utmost comfort.


Reza Fitness is an exclusive resort hotel gym that redefines the concept of personalized fitness experiences. Nestled within a picturesque setting, this state-of-the-art facility is dedicated to helping you achieve your fitness goals while providing a luxurious and inviting atmosphere.
